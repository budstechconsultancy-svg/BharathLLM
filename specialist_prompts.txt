SECTION B — LEGAL ENTITY EXTRACTOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Build /pipeline/legal_entity_extractor.py:

  extract_legal_entities(text: str) -> dict:
    Using regex + NLP, extract from any legal text:

    ACT REFERENCES:
      Pattern: r"(Section|S\.|Sec\.)\s*(\d+[A-Z]?)\s*(of\s+the\s+)?([A-Z][A-Za-z\s]+Act,?\s*\d{4})"
      Also: r"Article\s+(\d+[A-Z]?)\s+of\s+the\s+Constitution"
      Normalise: "Sec. 138 N.I. Act" → "Section 138, Negotiable Instruments Act 1881"

    CASE CITATIONS:
      Pattern: r"(\d{4})\s+(\d+)\s+(SCC|AIR|SCR|Cr\.?LJ|ILR)\s+(\d+)"
      Also: r"([A-Z][a-z]+\s+v\.?\s+[A-Z][a-z]+)\s*[\(\[]?(\d{4})[\)\]]?"
      e.g. "AIR 2019 SC 2014", "Indus Airways v. Magnum Aviation (2014)"

    LIMITATION PERIODS:
      Pattern: match "within \d+ (days|months|years)" near legal action words
      Map to: Limitation Act 1963 Article numbers

    LEGAL TOPICS (keyword classification):
      Criminal: murder, rape, theft, cheating, bail, FIR, cognisable
      Civil: contract, decree, injunction, specific performance, damages
      Family: divorce, maintenance, custody, succession, will
      Tax: GST, income tax, TDS, assessment, appeal, penalty
      Corporate: company, director, winding up, insolvency, NCLT
      Property: sale, lease, mortgage, partition, patta, encumbrance
      Constitutional: fundamental rights, writ, habeas corpus, mandamus
      Consumer: deficiency, unfair trade, redressal, compensation

    Return: {acts, citations, topics, limitation_refs, parties, court_names}

METHOD: search_judgements(query: str, filters: dict = None) -> list
------------------------------------------------------------
  Convert query to legal search:
    1. Extract entities from query using extract_legal_entities()
    2. Build enhanced search query:
         If sections mentioned: "Section {X} {Act} ratio decidendi judgement"
         If facts provided: use fact pattern + legal topic keywords
    3. Search legal_sc_judgements + relevant legal_hc_judgements_{state}
    4. Retrieve top 10 (more than standard RAG — judgements need wider net)
    5. POST-FILTER:
         Remove overruled judgements (is_overruled=True) — flag them separately
         Prioritise recent judgements (last 10 years) unless historical needed
         For BNS/BNSS queries: also include old IPC/CrPC judgements as applicable
    6. Return list of {case_name, citation, court, date, ratio, relevant_chunk,
                       is_overruled, overruled_by, relevance_score}

  SPECIAL: If query mentions old IPC section, auto-translate:
    "Section 302 IPC" → also search "Section 103 BNS" + cite mapping.

METHOD: calculate_limitation(case_type: str, trigger_date: str,
                               special_facts: str = None) -> dict
------------------------------------------------------------
  Limitation periods under Limitation Act 1963:
  COMMON_LIMITATIONS = {
    "money_suit": {"period": "3 years", "article": "113"},
    "cheque_bounce_138": {"period": "1 month from receipt of notice", "article": "N.I. Act S.142"},
    "consumer_complaint": {"period": "2 years from cause of action", "article": "Consumer Protection Act S.69"},
    "civil_appeal_sc": {"period": "90 days from decree", "article": "Limitation Act Art. 116"},
    "criminal_appeal_hc": {"period": "60 days from judgement", "article": "CrPC S.374/BNSS S.415"},
    "writ_petition": {"period": "No fixed limit — promptness required", "article": "Constitution A.226"},
    "property_suit": {"period": "12 years", "article": "Limitation Act Art. 65"},
    "mortgage_suit": {"period": "30 years", "article": "Limitation Act Art. 61"},
    "labour_dispute": {"period": "3 years", "article": "ID Act S.10(2A)"},
    "cheque_138_complaint": {"period": "1 month from 15th day after notice", "article": "N.I. Act S.142(b)"},
  }

  Calculate:
    expiry_date = trigger_date + period
    days_remaining = expiry_date - today
    If special_facts: check condonation grounds (illness, fraud, mistake of law)

  Return: {limitation_period, trigger_event, expiry_date, days_remaining,
           article_reference, condonation_possible, condonation_grounds}

  COMPANY CALCULATION:
    Domestic company rates (FY 2024-25):

  Return: {total_income, taxable_income, tax_before_cess,
